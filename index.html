Personality Psychology

A major topic of interest in psychology. Personality make a human what they are, it defines a certain persona. It is a scientific study which aims to show how people are individually different due to psychological forces

Social Psychology

Social psychologists seek to understand social behaviour, including how wer interact with others and how people influence our behaviour. It is a field that looks at a wide varaiety of social behaviours including topics such as the bystander effect, attitudes, and personal perception.

Furthermore, this can then impact the other people surrounding that person as well. Its almost like a natural circumstance where a habbit (good or bad) gets passed to the ones around you.
