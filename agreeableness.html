Agreeableness

By Safal Sapkota02 Aug 2019

Agreeableness is another vital personality trait. A person with a high level of agreeableness in a personality test is usually warm, friendly, and quite tactful. They generally have an optimistic view of human nature and get along really well with others.

Sub traits of agreeableness are as follows:
-Sympathy
-Modesty
-Trust
-Morality
-Co-operation
-Altruism
-Selfless

Agreeableness is considered an advantageous trait for building teams, strengthening relations and maintaining harmony on the work floor. People generally seem to like agreeable people rather than disagreeable ones because it's easy to form a healthy conversation at any time. 
On the other hand, agreeableness may not be useful in some occupational situations that requires difficult, unique or objective discussions. People who score low on agreeableness in a personality test often make excellent scientists, critics, or soldiers.
