Conscientiousness

By Safal Sapkota02 Aug 2019

Conscientiousness is an important ability that lies between individuals. A person scoring high in conscientiousness usually has a high level of self-discipline. These individuals prefer to follow a plan, rather than acting spontaneously. Their methodic planning and perseverance usually makes them highly successful in their chosen occupation. 

Sub traits of the conscientiousness domain are:
-Self efficacy
-Orderliness
-Dutifulness
-Achievement stirring
-Self discipline
-Cautiousness

A high score of conscientiousness in a career test is an important indicator of success. These individuals are dependable, organised and persevere, which means they will accomplish their professional goals.
